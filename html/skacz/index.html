About chess server

This is really free bughouse chess server. All are welcome to play and test lightning, blitz chess, crazyhouse and bughouse! Actually alpha version for testing. Time controls N minutes for game and also B seconds for move (bronstein) are supported. We hope soon here could be best bughouse chess actions. We do what we can to have really open source project working. We play not for rating, but for moves, games and tourneys ! But rating system here is supported also, its part of fun. Actually will be improved a bit, based on last years experiece. We plan organize here periodic online tournaments opened to all players without limitations of skills, rating, or whatever.

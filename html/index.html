About free internet chess server

This is really free bughouse internet chess server. All are welcome to play and test lightning, blitz chess, crazyhouse and bughouse! Whole family can play from single PC, students from same room. Or anybody from any place! Actually stable second beta version. Time controls N minutes for game and also B seconds for move (bronstein) are supported. We hope soon here could be best bughouse chess actions. We do what we can to have really open source project working. We play not for rating, but for moves, games and tourneys ! But rating system here is supported also, its part of fun. And rating for bughouse chess is improved based on last years experiece. We focusing on team and tournament rating, to better describe level of player's skills. We plan organize here periodic online tournaments opened to all players without limitations of skills, rating, or whatever.

Services & Server : Are Free

You can play here a lot of games for free. Register here. You can change using command password oldpassword newpassword. If You have some nick on FICS already, its reserved for You here also and for nobody else!

As interface to connect to our server You can use Thief or Winboard/Xboard interface. Also You can use multiplatform Raptor interface. If You are new, follow tutorial in pictures how to create new connection to ICS. Also Babas interface works somehow. If You wish play bughouse chess actually Thief is recommended now or winboard. We solving some issues for other interfaces.
